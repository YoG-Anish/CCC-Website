Supriya Tuladhar

Business Relationship Manager, ACCA Nepal

We were delighted to engage Mr. Rajesh Khadka, FCCA and Executive Coach, to lead a mindfulness session for our ACCA members. His expert guidance on neuroplasticity, mindfulness, and loving-kindness meditation greatly benefited our members by enhancing their focus, emotional well-being, and resilience. This session has been instrumental in supporting their personal and professional development, positively impacting their overall performance. From our experience, the training not only enriched the well-being of our members but also fostered a more mindful and balanced community within our organisation. We truly appreciate Rajesh’s impactful contribution and look forward to more valuable sessions in future.
